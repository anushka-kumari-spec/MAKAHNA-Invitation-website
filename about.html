The Makhana story

Inspired by a timeless superfood from India.

Makhana, also known as fox nuts, is a nutritious seed harvested from the lotus plant and celebrated across India, especially in Bihar, for its light texture and rich nutritional value. It has long been appreciated in traditional wellness and cuisine for its natural goodness, versatility, and cultural significance.

At UDYAMkart, we draw inspiration from that story of purity, quality, and heritage. Just as makhana stands for value and tradition, our brand is built around trust, growth, and a modern path forward.
